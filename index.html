Welcome to Trezor.io/Start®

Learn how to set up your Trezor hardware wallet with our detailed guide. The Trezor.io/start (Version 22.9.3) offers a streamlined process for secure crypto management. Follow these steps to get started:

1. Initial Setup

Begin by connecting your Trezor wallet to your computer. Visit Trezor.io/start to download the necessary software.

2. Creating a Secure PIN

For security, you'll need to set up a PIN. Ensure it's strong and unique to protect your crypto assets.

3. Backup Your Recovery Seed

Write down your recovery seed and store it in a safe place. This is crucial for recovering your wallet in case of loss or theft.

4. Sync Your Wallet

Follow the on-screen instructions to sync your Trezor wallet with your Trezor Suite. This ensures all your data is up-to-date.

5. Start Using Your Wallet

Once setup is complete, you can start managing your crypto assets securely. For more information, visit our Trezor Start page.
